# Button appearance

> **Status: TODO.** Notion source has bullets but no finalised guidance. Treat the points below as drafts and escalate to `@design` for anything binding.

## Draft notes from Notion

- Only a single primary button per screen.
- A gray button sits next to the primary button.
- Icon buttons (with tooltips) are for actions that aren't used often.

## Open questions

- When does "secondary" become a third primary?

- Sizing pairings across `Button` and `IconButton` (`size="1"` vs `"2"`).
